##### Premise



game world can be rotated and "hinged" to move all characters and objects

the player can choose to rotate by 15,30,45 degrees, corresponding to different move distances

a hinge axis can be selected and the level splits causing some to move one way, and some the other

the goal is to get all players to the end of the level

enemies are not required to be killed to progress



enemies cannot be directly fought unless they are rammed

the player can also damage enemies or players by accident if an object/character runs into another, with higher speed doing more damage

at the end of your turn, your players and the enemies attack each other with their respective moveset



How will they attack?

* closest opponent
* lowest opponent
* strongest opponent
* some kind of smart targeting
* player choice of above



players have limited abilities, that take place during the move phase:

* fortify: cant move and take reduced damage
* hop: jump to the target location and gain a damage buff
* reverse: move in the opposite direction and deal double ram damage
